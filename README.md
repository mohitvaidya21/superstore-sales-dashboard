# Super Store Sales Dashboard | Power BI Project

## Turning Raw Sales Data into Business Insights

In many businesses, sales data is available in large amounts, but it is often difficult to understand what the data is actually saying. Numbers alone do not clearly show which region is performing better, which category is generating more profit, or where the business is losing opportunities.

This project was created to solve that problem.

Using Super Store sales data, I built an interactive Power BI dashboard that transforms raw data into meaningful insights. The goal was to make business performance easier to understand and help decision-makers quickly identify patterns, trends, and problem areas.

---

## The Problem

A business may have thousands of sales records, but without proper visualization, it becomes hard to answer important questions such as:

- Which region is generating the highest sales?
- Which category and sub-category are performing well?
- Are high sales also leading to high profit?
- Which shipping mode is used the most?
- How are sales and profit changing over time?
- Which states need more business attention?

When data is stored only in tables or spreadsheets, these questions take time to answer and important insights may be missed.

---

## The Solution

To address this, I developed a Power BI dashboard that presents the sales data in a visual and interactive format.

The dashboard helps in:

- tracking overall sales, profit, quantity, and shipping days
- comparing performance across regions, segments, and states
- analyzing category-wise and sub-category-wise sales
- understanding monthly sales and profit trends
- identifying preferred shipping modes and payment modes
- supporting faster and better business decisions

Instead of reading rows of raw data, users can now understand the story behind the data through charts, KPIs, and maps.

---

## Dashboard Highlights

This dashboard includes:

- KPI cards for Sales, Quantity, Profit, and Ship Days
- Sales analysis by Region
- Sales analysis by Segment
- Sales analysis by Payment Mode
- Monthly Sales by Year
- Monthly Profit by Year
- Sales by Ship Mode
- Sales by Category and Sub-Category
- State-wise Sales and Profit visualization
